Book Review

In my quest for fun & great networking resources,
How the internet Really works is arguably one of the best starting points!
The creators give a visual overview of how the internet works; from your home to the world, stakeholders involved ; and how censorship and tech giants influence us. Even though its not your typical networking book, you couldn't start better!

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Rated Giga on the chad scale
